shmlv is my one-person audio tools brand. I started it because a lot of the problems that slow producers down are not huge, glamorous problems — they're the tiny workflow gaps you hit every day when you're editing MIDI, building arrangements, and trying to move fast without breaking your focus.

So instead of waiting for someone else to fix them, I build the tools myself.

I focus on practical music-production tools: scripts, plugins, presets, templates, and other utilities that do one useful job well. No bloated feature lists. No fake company voice. No product committee trying to turn a simple idea into a platform.

Everything here is built by one developer, tested in real use, and made for producers who care about speed, clarity, and getting back to making music.

The name? shmlv = Shumilov, minus the vowels. Keep it short, keep it sharp.

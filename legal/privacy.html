Privacy Policy

EFFECTIVE · APRIL 2 · 2026·v1.0

shmlv is operated by Denis Shumilov, an independent developer based in Ukraine ("shmlv", "I", "me", "my").

01What I collect

a. Website data

This site currently does not load advertising pixels, tracking cookies, or third-party font files. It may embed YouTube videos only after you choose to play them; YouTube may then receive technical data such as IP address, browser details, and request logs. The hosting provider may also process basic technical data to keep the site running and secure.

b. Order and billing data

Payments are currently processed through Payhip, a third-party checkout provider. When you buy a Product, Payhip and I may process data such as your name, email address, country, transaction identifiers, the Product purchased, and tax information.

c. Support data

If you contact me for support, I may receive the information you send, including your name, email, order details, DAW and OS information, message content, and any screenshots or files you choose to share.

d. Optional marketing data

I do not send newsletters or marketing emails unless you clearly opt in.

02How I use your data

  • Deliver Products and confirm purchases
  • Confirm purchases, handle license or refund questions, and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Provide customer support and troubleshoot problems
  • Send service messages about downloads, updates, and order issues
  • Keep accounting, tax, legal, and dispute-resolution records

I do not sell your personal data. I do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling.

03How data is shared

I only share data when needed to run the business or comply with law. That may include Payhip (for checkout, billing, tax, receipts, and refunds), the hosting provider, email providers, YouTube embeds you choose to play, and professional advisers or authorities where required.

04Retention

I keep purchase and billing records for as long as reasonably needed for tax, accounting, and legal purposes. Support emails are kept as long as needed to resolve your request.

05Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, email support@shmlv.com.

06Cookies and tracking

I do not use advertising cookies. If I enable analytics later, I will update this Policy before doing so. Third-party services such as Payhip and YouTube may use their own cookies or similar technologies under their own policies.

07Children's privacy

shmlv Products are not directed to children. Do not submit personal data or make purchases if you are under the required age in your country.

08Changes

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version posted on the site applies from its stated effective date.

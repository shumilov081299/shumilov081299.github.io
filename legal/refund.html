Refund Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Digital Products

All sales of digital products are final due to the nature of downloadable software. Once a product is purchased and the download link is provided, the transaction is considered complete.

When We Issue Refunds

We may issue a refund if the product does not function as described in the product listing. To request a refund, you must contact us within 14 days of purchase with a clear description of the issue, including your DAW version, operating system, and steps to reproduce the problem.

Refund Process

Refund requests are reviewed on a case-by-case basis. We may ask for additional information or screenshots to verify the reported issue. If the issue is confirmed, a full refund will be processed through Paddle within 5-10 business days.

Not Eligible for Refund

Refunds will not be issued for change of mind, failure to read the product description or compatibility requirements, or use on unsupported platforms.
